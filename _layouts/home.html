About Me

My name is Fotios Kounelis and I am a PhD student in the Large-Scale Distributed Systems (LSDS) group at Imperial College London, under the supervision of Dr. Holger Pirk. Prior to this, I acquired my Engineers degree and my MSc in the University of Patras, in the Computer Engineering and Informatics Department and conducted both of my thesis under the supervision of Dr. Christos Makris.

My PhD research focuses on program rewriting using compiler transformations for data-intensive applications. More specifically, I work on GraalVM, a JVM Java-writen compiler, creating a rewriting framework that optimizes data-intensive programms by applying tranformations based on compression techniques.

My research interests lies in the areas of Compiler Optimizations and Compression Techniques but I am also interested in the areas of Bioinformatics, Information Retrieval and Data Structures.
